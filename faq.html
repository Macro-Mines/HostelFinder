Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about using HostelFinder.

How do I search for a hostel?

To search for a hostel, simply select your desired university from the dropdown menu on the homepage and click "Find Hostels". You can then use the filters on the left to narrow down your results by gender, rent range, distance, and facilities.

Is HostelFinder free to use?

Yes, HostelFinder is completely free for students to use. Our goal is to connect students with the best accommodation options without any hidden charges or subscription fees.

How can hostel owners list their property?

Hostel owners can easily list their properties by visiting the "Owner" section from the homepage. Fill out the simple form with your hostel's details, and it will be added to our listings after verification.

What information is provided in a hostel listing?

Each hostel listing includes detailed information such as hostel name, location, distance from university, gender specific (Boys/Girls/Unisex), room rent range, available room capacities (Single, Double, Triple), facilities (AC, Wi-Fi, Mess, Laundry), and direct contact details for the owner.

How accurate is the distance from university?

The distances provided are approximate and are meant to give you a general idea. We recommend verifying the exact distance and commute time directly with the hostel owner.

Can I contact the hostel owner directly?

Yes, every hostel listing provides the owner's contact name, phone number, and email address. You can reach out to them directly for inquiries, bookings, or more detailed information.

What if I find outdated information?

We strive to keep our listings as current as possible. If you encounter any outdated or incorrect information, please report it to us via the "Contact Us" page, and we will rectify it promptly.

Are there hostels for both boys and girls?

Yes, our platform includes hostels that are 'Boys Only', 'Girls Only', and 'Unisex'. You can easily filter your search based on gender specificity to find suitable options.

How often are new hostels added?

We are continuously working to expand our network. New hostels are added regularly as owners register their properties and pass our verification process. Check back frequently for updated listings!

I am an owner, and I want to update my hostel details. How?

Currently, for updates to existing listings, please contact our support team directly via the "Contact Us" page, providing your hostel's name and the details you wish to change. We are working on an owner dashboard for self-service updates.
